Privacy Policy for Direx

Last Updated: 8th June, 2025

This Discord bot (Direx) operates with utmost respect for your privacy. Direx does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

How Direx Operates (No Data Collection)

  • The Bot operates entirely within Discord's environment, focusing purely on in-server functionality.
  • Unlike many bots, Direx is designed to not collect or log messages, usernames, or any personal identifying information from users.
  • Direx functions without the use of any external databases or tracking systems, ensuring your interactions remain private.

Data Handling: What Happens When You Use Direx?

Any data processed by Direx (such as temporary message content for command parsing, or Discord User/Guild IDs to deliver a command's response) is used ephemerally in-memory for functionality and is immediately discarded. It is absolutely not stored persistently on any disk or database.

Third-Party Data Sharing: None

Direx upholds a strict "no sharing" policy. The Bot does not share any data with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics platforms.
